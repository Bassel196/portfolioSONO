About the Project

Sonocardia is an intelligent heart sound analysis system that uses deep learning to detect heart murmurs and predict detailed murmur characteristics. Our system provides accurate patient-level analysis based on audio recordings from multiple auscultation points, helping to improve early heart disease detection.

Why Sonocardia?

Cardiovascular diseases claim 17.9 million lives annually — that's 32% of all global deaths. Sonocardia is revolutionizing cardiac care through real-time, AI-driven heart monitoring.

What is Sonocardia?

An AI-powered mobile application that enables real-time heart sound and ECG analysis to detect and predict heart diseases quickly and accurately, using embedded hardware.

How It Works

  • Record: 5–10 seconds of heart sound and ECG via the app
  • Analyze: ML & DL models trained on 100k+ real-world samples
  • Diagnose: Classify as normal/abnormal, predict severity, alert user
  • Share: Instantly send reports to physicians or caregivers

Technology Highlights

Machine & Deep Learning (CNNs, Random Forest, XGBoost)
Accuracy: Averaging +90% in HeartSound Detection, Severity Classification and early Arrhythmia risk Prediction
Integrated hardware for real-time recording & feedback
Captures heart sounds using a modified stethoscope MAX9814 sensor & records ECG signals via AD8232 sensor
The recordings saved in local SD Card then it tranfers to the database by ESP-32 micro-controller
Seamless cloud communication and data visualization
Built-in security & emergency response capabilities
